COVID19 prevention

Always take care of these things to get prevented from COVID19 virus.

1. Wash your hand regularly for 20sec with soap and water or alchohal based hand-rub...

2. Maintain a safe distance from others (at least 1 metre), even if they don’t appear to be sick.

3. Wear a mask in public, especially indoors or when physical distancing is not possible.

4. Get vaccinated when it’s your turn. Follow local guidance about vaccination.
